United CNC — Account & Data Deletion

At United CNC, we value your privacy and control over your personal data. If you wish to permanently delete your user account and all associated information stored on our servers, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered account username to: sridakshyani.apps@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: [United CNC] Account Deletion Request
  3. In the body of the email, state your full name and confirm your registered email identifier.
Launch Deletion Email

Data Handling, Deletion & Retention Policy

  • What is permanently deleted: Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently purge your user profile, authentication credentials, saved preferences, and camera uploads from our production relational databases and cloud buckets.
  • What data is kept: Historical log activities containing strictly aggregated, non-personally identifiable technical telemetry may be retained for analytical performance optimizations.
  • Retention and processing period: Deletion processing will be completed within 7 business days from receipt of your verified email. Once executed, this action is entirely irreversible.
Important Note: If you currently have an active machine maintenance agreement or unresolved service engineer transactions tied to your profile, please coordinate with corporate support prior to initiating your account deletion.
